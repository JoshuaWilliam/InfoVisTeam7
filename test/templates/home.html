Scatter Plot

This introductory visualization displays viewers sentiment means across a few popular subreddits. It shows the attitude of people under speficic sentiment classes which can be distinguished by their colors.

Multiview


The focus of this multiview is the chord diagram, displaying a network of the subreddits with its relational properties.
The linked visualizations consist of a bar chart for LIWC comparison between two subreddits as well as a scatterplot indicating the clusters where subreddits with similar sentiment and topics fall into.
